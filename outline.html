Introduction

Introduce the current situation of hospital management and the crisis it faces, leading to agile methods. Introduce the structure of the paper, leading to the core challenge. Problem Formulation Define NGS, agile, brief analysis of hospital status, and introduce the possible role of agile in hospital management and hospital data analysis.

Current Strategies

Introduced from the agile management and agile data analysis phases. 1. Agile Management For hospitals, their primary task is to provide patients with the right level and quality of care, to understand the patient and health plan members and the cost of health care services. Analysis of why Agile will help hospital management. Agile has some disadvantages in hospital management. 2. Agile Data Analysis As with agile software development, agile data analysis is built on a set of core values and guiding principles. Agile analysis is a way of building data warehouses, data marts, business intelligence applications, and analysis programs. Agile analysis values: - Individuals and interactions over processes and tools - Working DW/BI systems over comprehensive documentation - End-user and stakeholder collaboration over contract negotiation - Responding to change over following a plan

In the hospital system, a large number of patients and diagnostic data are always faced. The analysis of these data is a challenging operation.Using agile data analysis methods can effectively improve the efficiency of data analysis.

Recommendations

Challenges that may be encountered in hospital agile management: 1. Agile team composition 2. How to change management mode. Hospital agile management does not have mature success stories that can be used for reference, but in other similar fields, such as banks, government and other institutions. I can summarize the relevant experience of hospital agile management by analyzing these institutions.
